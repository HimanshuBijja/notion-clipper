# Privacy Policy for Notion Clipper

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## Overview

Notion Clipper is a browser extension that helps you save selected text from webpages to your Notion workspace. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Nothing.** This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### What Stays on Your Device
- **Notion Integration Token:** Stored locally in your browser's secure storage
- **Root Page ID:** Stored locally in your browser's secure storage
- **Last Used Path:** Stored locally to remember your preferred save location

## Data Transmission

The extension only communicates with:
- **Notion's Official API** (`api.notion.com`) - to save your clips

No data is ever sent to any other server, analytics service, or third party.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `storage` | Save your settings locally |
| `activeTab` | Read selected text from the current page |
| `scripting` | Get the selected text with proper formatting |
| `contextMenus` | Add "Save to Notion" to right-click menu |
| `notifications` | Show success/error messages |
| `host_permissions: api.notion.com` | Send clips to Notion |

## Your Control

- All data is stored locally on your device
- You can clear all extension data by removing the extension
- You can view/modify stored data in the extension's Options page

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses:
- **Notion API** - Subject to [Notion's Privacy Policy](https://www.notion.so/privacy)

## Open Source

This extension's source code is available for review. You can verify exactly what the extension does.
